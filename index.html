My Favorite Memory

When I was a boy, my father and I would regularly take a short trip to the country on the weekend. This was a routine for us because it helped us away from the noisy and busy city live. We would walk into the woods, surrounded by tall green trees, with thier leaves covered in dew. The birds chirping high up in the trees, creating a well ochestrated symphony. The sunrays beaming down through the leaves and branches. We would pluck fresh and ripe fruits while walking down the pathway that winds through the evergreen trees. Once in a while, we would a wild squirrel foraging for seeds and nuts.
